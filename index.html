About

I've been camping since I was a kid, but now I'm the person in charge of booking the campsites I visit with family and friends. It's difficult to choose the right campsite, and to be frank, there are some campsites out there that are flat out bad compared to others.

So I wanted to create a personal logger where you can log your favourite sites and the ones you'd rather avoid. When you have free time out camping, you can take a nice walk around the campground and jot down which sites you'd like to book next, give it a rating, and so on.

It's a nice excuse for a walk around the campgrounds, and it gives you ease knowing which campsite to book next time and not be surprised with a funky site.
